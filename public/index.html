Visualizing DNA Sequences


This visualization was created as a visual aid to detect errors in DNA sequences by comparing them against known sequences and computing a score. This score represents how similar they are to known sequences. The higher the score, the more similar they are to known sequences and therefore less likely to be erroneous.

Click on "Filter DNA Sequences" and select sequences you would like to see to get started.
